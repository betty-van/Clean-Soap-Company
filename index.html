About Clean Soap Co.

Here at the Clean Soap Company, we pride ourselves in selling high quality and environmental-friendly products.

We are a small family company originating in the Pacific Northwest. Our team consists of a husband, wife, and two teenagers. Soap-making has been a long household tradition. It's fun, practical, and a great way to get our creative juices flowing.
